# Model Card for "PrivacyShield NLP Analyzer"

## Overview
The "PrivacyShield NLP Analyzer" is a model designed to assist users in assessing privacy and GDPR compliance in textual documents. It is developed and maintained by PrivacyShield Technologies Ltd.

---

## 1. Consent Management (Articles 6, 7)

We ensure that any personal data processing based on consent meets GDPR standards:
- Consent is requested explicitly before any data processing occurs.
- Consent records are securely maintained and time-stamped.
- Users may withdraw consent at any time through their account settings or by contacting our Data Protection Officer (DPO).

---

## 2. Data Anonymization (Articles 5, 25)

All input data is immediately anonymized upon receipt:
- Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is masked during pre-processing.
- Only anonymized text is used for model inference.
- No original (identifiable) input data is stored after session end.

---

## 3. Policy Updates (Articles 12, 13)

We regularly review and update our privacy policy:
- Users are notified of any material changes via email and in-app banners 30 days before changes take effect.
- A changelog of previous policy versions is publicly available on our website.
- Users are given the opportunity to accept or decline significant policy updates.

_Last updated: April 2025_

---

## 4. Data Subject Rights (Articles 12–23)

We fully support user rights under the GDPR, including:
- Right to Access
- Right to Rectification
- Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
- Right to Restriction of Processing
- Right to Data Portability
- Right to Object

Users can submit requests by emailing **dpo@privacyshield.ai**. All requests are processed within **14 days** in compliance with updated GDPR timelines.

---

## 5. Data Breach Procedures (Articles 33, 34)

We maintain an incident response plan:
- All breaches are assessed within 24 hours of detection.
- If personal data is involved, users and authorities are notified within 72 hours as required.
- Breach details and mitigation steps are logged in an internal breach register.

---

## 6. Third-Party Data Processing (Articles 28, 32)

Our subprocessors (such as cloud providers or AI hosting platforms) undergo annual GDPR compliance audits.  
Key protections include:
- Signed Data Processing Agreements (DPAs)
- Regular verification of technical and organizational security measures
- Subprocessors must notify us of any subprocessing or breaches immediately.

---

## 7. International Data Transfers (Articles 44–50)

Data is primarily processed within the European Economic Area (EEA). Where necessary, transfers to third countries are protected by:
- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
- Adequacy decisions by the European Commission
